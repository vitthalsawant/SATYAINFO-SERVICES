about us

SATYAINFO SERVICES - Experts in X-Ray Technology

SATYAINFO SERVICES is a Bhubaneswar (Odisha) based provider of diagnostic X-ray equipment and medical imaging solutions for hospitals, nursing homes, clinics, and diagnostic centres across Odisha and India. We supply Fixed X-ray systems, Mobile X-ray units, Digital Radiography solutions, OPG/CBCT machines, and essential accessories—backed by practical guidance from selection to commissioning.

Our focus is simple: dependable image quality, safe operation, and long-term uptime. From site assessment and installation to user training and after-sales service, we support your team with professional sales and service assistance so you can deliver faster, more confident diagnosis and better patient care.

Led by Samir Ranjan Panda, our team brings hands-on experience in medical imaging equipment and a customer-first approach—clear communication, transparent recommendations, and support you can rely on.

  • Consultation to match equipment with your clinical workload & budget
  • Support for site readiness, installation coordination & commissioning
  • Operator guidance and training for smooth day-one usage
  • Preventive maintenance and responsive service support
  • Accessories and spares to keep your workflow uninterrupted
  • Long-term partnership focused on reliability and value

about us

Welcome to SATYAINFO SERVICES

SATYAINFO SERVICES is a trusted provider of high-quality diagnostic X-ray equipment and medical imaging solutions, based in Bhubaneswar, Odisha. We specialize in offering comprehensive solutions for healthcare facilities, including fixed X-ray systems, mobile X-ray units, digital radiography systems, OPG/CBCT machines, and essential accessories. Our commitment is to deliver superior performance, reliable service support, and cost-effective solutions that meet international quality standards. With expertise in X-ray technology and a focus on customer satisfaction, SATYAINFO SERVICES serves healthcare institutions across Odisha and India, ensuring they have access to advanced diagnostic imaging equipment backed by professional sales and service support.
